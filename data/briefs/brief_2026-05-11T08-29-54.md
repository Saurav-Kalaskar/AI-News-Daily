# TL;DR
A recent discovery by Mythos, a company that generates training data for AI models, has raised concerns about the security of AI training data. The company found a known CVE (Common Vulnerability and Exposure) in its training data, highlighting the potential risks of using unvetted data to train AI models.

## Key Releases
- Mythos discovers a CVE in its training data, sparking concerns about AI data security.

## Developer Takeaways
- The discovery emphasizes the importance of thoroughly vetting and securing AI training data to prevent potential security risks.
- Developers should prioritize data validation and testing to ensure the integrity of their AI models.
- The use of unvetted data can lead to unintended consequences, including the propagation of vulnerabilities.
